History

The start

The band got together for the first time to make magic in 1986. The band was then only Link, Zelda and a dream.
Since then they have picked up friend along the journey and continues to evolve in their creation of soundmagic.

The first album

The first album, Highway to Hyrule was an immediate hit when it released in 1998. The world was fascinated and could not get enough, the album sold platinum in multiple countries.
The album is often referred to as a classic and is among many top 10 lists still to this day.

The new EP

After a long hiatus the band recently released a new EP with 6 brand new songs. Since that they have gone viral and also sold out tickets for the start of the Europe-tour, where the first show is in Linköping.
Now the world waits with high anticipation to see how they will perform live again.
